Frequently Asked Questions

What types of doors do you manufacture?

We specialize in a wide range of doors including premium Teak Wood doors, 100% waterproof WPC doors, and stylish Laminate doors in various designs and finishes.

Do you offer custom sizes?

Yes, absolutely. We are a factory-based manufacturer and can create doors in custom sizes to perfectly fit your requirements. Please mention your size requirements when you contact us for a quote.

What is the warranty on your doors?

Warranty varies by material. Our WPC doors come with a lifetime warranty against water and termite damage, while our wooden doors have a warranty covering structural integrity. Please check the specific product page for details.

What is your delivery timeline?

Our standard delivery time within Bengaluru is typically 7-10 working days. This may vary for highly customized orders. We will provide a precise timeline when you place your order.
