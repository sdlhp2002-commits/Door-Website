Our Story & Vision

Ajor Doors is a trusted name in premium door manufacturing, offering high-quality, stylish, and durable door solutions for homes, offices, and commercial spaces. With a strong commitment to craftsmanship and innovation, we design doors that combine modern aesthetics with long-lasting performance.

For years, we have supplied a wide range of wooden doors, laminated doors, flush doors, PVC doors, and customized designs to customers across India. Every door is crafted using advanced manufacturing technology, high-grade raw materials, and strict quality standards to ensure superior strength, smooth finishes, and long life.

Our Journey

2002

Inception

Ajor Doors was founded with a vision to provide high-quality wooden doors to local builders in Bengaluru.

2008

Expansion

Expanded our manufacturing unit and introduced Teak wood customization options.

2015

New Materials

Launched our premium range of WPC (Wood Polymer Composite) doors for waterproof solutions.

2023

Digital Era

Opened our flagship showroom and launched digital printing technology for door designs.
