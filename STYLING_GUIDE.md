## ğŸ–¼ï¸ Image Improvements

### General Image Enhancements:
1. **Borders & Frames**
   - Add subtle borders (2-3px, matching theme)
   - Rounded corners (8-12px border-radius)
   - Inner shadows for depth

2. **Shadows**
   - Soft drop shadows (0 10px 30px rgba(0,0,0,0.1))
   - Layered shadows for premium feel
   - Colored shadows matching theme

3. **Hover Effects**
   - Smooth zoom (scale 1.05-1.1)
   - Shadow enhancement on hover
   - Brightness/contrast adjustments
   - Overlay effects

4. **Loading States**
   - Skeleton loaders
   - Blur-up technique
   - Progressive image loading

5. **Image Presentation**
   - Consistent aspect ratios
   - Proper object-fit (contain/cover)
   - Lazy loading (already implemented)
   - Responsive sizing
